About Us

Welcome to Tapti Green Fruits Suppliers – Fresh Fruits from Burhanpur, Madhya Pradesh

Tapti Green Fruits Suppliers is a leading name in fresh fruit supply, based in Burhanpur, Madhya Pradesh (PIN: 450331). Established in 2021 under the vision of Mr. Farhan Patel, we specialize in supplying Banana, Watermelon, Muskmelon, Papaya, and other seasonal fruits across India.

With over 4 years of industry experience, we are committed to delivering premium-quality fruits directly from farms to your doorstep.

We take pride in our advanced testing and R&D facilities, which help us maintain consistent fruit quality and shelf life. Whether you are a wholesaler, retailer, or distributor, Tapti Green Fruits Suppliers is your trusted partner for fresh, reliable fruit supply.

3. Competitive Pricing: We offer attractive wholesale prices without compromising on quality, helping you maximize your profits.

6. Experienced & Dedicated Team: Led by industry professionals with years of expertise, we prioritize customer satisfaction and responsive support.
